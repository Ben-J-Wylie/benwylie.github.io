Mr. Goodman's Manor

2006

Synopsis

Mr. Goodman's Manor invites audiences into the lavish yet enigmatic world of a very rich man who opens the doors of his grand, mysterious mansion for a unique guided tour.

As a group of curious tourists explores the vast manor, they are led through each room, where the decor is described in poetic detail by a charismatic tour guide.

With each room, a new chapter of the manorâ€™s history unfolds, setting the stage for a series of mesmerizing performance vignettes. These vignettes feature imaginative, genre-defying acts, including captivating dance performances, soaring opera singers, and dynamic circle-style athletes, each adding to the mystique and charm of the manor.

As the live performances progress, the backdrop evolves slowly through stunning visual projections, immersing the audience in a dreamlike, ever-changing environment.

Mr. Goodman's Manor combines visual artistry, live performance, and narrative poetry to create an unforgettable theatrical experience that invites audiences to explore the boundaries of imagination, beauty, and mystery.
