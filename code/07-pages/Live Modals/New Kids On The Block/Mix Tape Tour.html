New Kids On The Block - Mix Tape Tour

2019

Project Scope

The New Kids on the Block, a beloved boy-band from the 90s, continue to have a huge fan base. When they embarked on their first tour in years, NKOTB aimed to create a distinctive concert experience. They introduced a 'mix-tape' approach with supporting artists and a randomized song setlist, along with various engaging fan opportunities.

Among these, APPIX stood out as the most significant success.

Our Approach

In the realm of concerts, the challenge of maintaining cellular data and Wi-Fi connectivity is substantial. Enter the APPIX patented and patents-pending broadcast platform; an ideal solution to connect with unlimited fans within each venue, night after night.

At every concert, for 52 performances, APPIX treated fans to an array of distinctive engagements. These included notifications and alerts on locked screens, as well as dynamic full-screen, full-color visuals and lively motion graphics, all impeccably synchronized with the music and spectacle the show became.

By the tour's end, APPIX had not only surpassed all expectations, particularly in terms of audience adoption - often exceeding 50% - but NKOTB also successfully offset the entire APPIX cost. This was accomplished through a truly innovative, entirely new revenue-generating approach that the APPIX platform provides.
