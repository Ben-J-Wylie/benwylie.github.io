Herbalife - Honors

2024

Project Scope

For over 40 years, Herbalife has cemented its reputation as a global forerunner in nutrition. With a rich portfolio of products ranging from meal replacements to dietary supplements, they've touched lives in 95 countries through a network of more than 6 million dedicated distributors or members.

Every year, Herbalife celebrates its elite distributors with a luxurious three-day event in Los Angeles. With attendees hailing from various corners of the world, communicating event details, navigating the city, and catering to diverse linguistic needs posed a significant challenge.

When Herbalife sought a solution for seamless communication, APPIX stepped in with its pioneering messaging service. Our goal was simple yet ambitious: Make the Herbalife LA extravaganza an unparalleled experience for every member attending.

Our Approach

Collaborating with Herbalife’s logistics and production teams, we designed bespoke messaging campaigns to cater to over fifty different groups, ensuring content relevance across a dozen languages.

Throughout the event, we dispatched over 1300 messages. This included both pre-scheduled notifications and real-time messages, making sure attendees were always in the loop.

All of this was made possible with APPIX’s robust, proprietary messaging platform. Whether it was guiding distributors through LA's landmarks or updating them about event schedules, our platform ensured timely and accurate communication.
