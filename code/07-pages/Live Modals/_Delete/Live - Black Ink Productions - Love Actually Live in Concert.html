Black Ink Productions - Love Actually Live in Concert

2021

Project Scope

In 2021, Black Ink Productions staged Love Actually - Live in Concert, a unique cinematic and orchestral experience that brought the beloved holiday film to life. The production featured a live orchestra performing the film’s score while the movie was projected on a large screen, immersing audiences in a multi-sensory spectacle.

To elevate audience engagement, APPIX was commissioned to integrate a dynamic second-screen experience, providing rich media messaging cues before the show, during intermission, and after the performance. Additionally, APPIX was tasked with ensuring real-time execution of cues despite significant logistical and budgetary constraints due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Our Approach

Given the challenges presented by the pandemic and financial constraints, APPIX implemented an innovative remote-controlled deployment strategy. For the first time in its history, APPIX executed all cue activations remotely from Vancouver, Canada, eliminating the need for on-site hardware.

Key technological solutions included:

  • Remote Cue Execution: All APPIX cues were triggered remotely via a secure connection, ensuring a seamless integration with the live performance.

  • Unity ClearCom Integration: A direct communication link was established between APPIX's remote operator and the stage manager through Unity ClearCom, allowing precise synchronization with live cue calls.

  • WebSocket Transmission: Rather than using traditional broadcast antenna hardware, all cues were transmitted to the on-site audience through WebSocket technology, providing a reliable and low-latency experience.

  • Pre-Show, Intermission, and Post-Show Engagement: Media-rich messaging was delivered to enhance audience interaction and engagement throughout the event.

Results & Impact

The execution of Love Actually - Live in Concert marked a milestone for APPIX, demonstrating the platform's ability to operate remotely and still deliver an immersive and interactive experience. Key outcomes included:

  • Flawless Remote Execution: Despite the complexity of remote operation, the event ran smoothly, proving the feasibility of APPIX’s remote activation model.

  • Enhanced Audience Experience: Attendees engaged with pre-show and intermission content seamlessly, creating a more immersive concert atmosphere.

  • Operational Efficiency: By eliminating on-site hardware requirements, APPIX reduced logistical costs and complexities without compromising performance quality.

  • Industry First: This was APPIX’s first fully remote production, setting a precedent for future remote-controlled deployments in live entertainment.

Conclusion

The success of Love Actually - Live in Concert showcased the adaptability and reliability of APPIX’s event technology. By leveraging remote execution capabilities, APPIX delivered an innovative, cost-effective solution that not only met but exceeded expectations. The project reinforced APPIX’s standing as a leader in audience engagement solutions, proving that live event interactivity can be achieved seamlessly, even in constrained and remote environments.
