Kiss POC

2021

Project Scope

In 2021, the iconic rock band KISS secured a highly anticipated Las Vegas residency at a premier venue. However, due to an unfortunate and deeply personal circumstance affecting one of the band members, they ultimately had to decline the residency. Prior to this unexpected change, APPIX was approached to explore how its technology could make the residency an unparalleled, immersive concert experience. APPIX was envisioned as a core component of the show, providing a high-impact audience engagement system that would evolve dynamically throughout the concert. The project aimed to create a permanent installation within the venue, ensuring that each night of the residency offered a seamlessly integrated, visually stunning, and interactive fan experience. The system needed to be robust, adaptable, and capable of responding to real-time changes in the show’s setlist and production cues.

Our Approach

To bring the vision to life, APPIX developed an innovative, multi-layered engagement platform with several key elements: Permanent Venue Integration: Unlike previous temporary activations, this residency was intended as a permanent APPIX installation, ensuring that the technology could be deeply embedded within the venue’s infrastructure. Evolving Audience Interaction: APPIX designed a real-time UI experience that would reveal new interactive elements as the concert unfolded. This included dynamic lighting effects, crowd-synced visuals, and exclusive second-screen content that complemented the band’s legendary pyrotechnics and stage theatrics. Setlist Agility: Given that KISS often adapts their setlist to audience energy and special circumstances, APPIX ensured its platform could flexibly respond to changes. The system was designed to allow real-time adjustments to interactive elements without disrupting the flow of the performance. Venue-Wide Synchronization: APPIX planned to integrate its technology seamlessly with the venue’s lighting, video, and sound systems, ensuring perfect synchronization between stage production and audience engagement.

Results & Impact

Although KISS ultimately did not proceed with the residency, the groundwork laid by APPIX provided a compelling proof of concept for permanent venue integrations. The project demonstrated APPIX’s capability to: Enhance live entertainment through interactive second-screen experiences, elevating audience participation to a new level. Implement a robust, venue-wide installation that seamlessly integrates with existing production technologies. Adapt dynamically to live event changes, proving its flexibility for artists with evolving setlists. This initiative reinforced APPIX’s reputation as a leader in audience engagement technology, showcasing the potential for future residencies and long-term concert integrations.

Results & Impact

While the KISS residency was ultimately canceled, this proof of concept demonstrated the power of APPIX’s platform in transforming live music experiences. The project laid the foundation for future collaborations with other artists, residencies, and permanent venue installations. By proving its ability to deliver high-impact, immersive audience engagement at scale, APPIX solidified its position as a game-changer in the live entertainment industry.
