Highlands Christmas

2022

Project Scope

In 2022, Highlands Church in Birmingham, Alabama, orchestrated an extraordinary live Christmas concert across 18 locations simultaneously. This massive production required seamless coordination to ensure a unified experience for all attendees, regardless of their location. To enhance audience engagement, APPIX was brought on board to provide synchronized second-screen content. This included: Supplementary video material Sing-along lyrics Important localized messaging Dynamic content synchronized with live talent features on stage This implementation ensured that every audience member across all 18 locations experienced a rich and immersive Christmas production.

Our Approach

Successfully deploying APPIX for this large-scale production required meticulous planning and collaboration with the Highlands Church production teams. Several key challenges had to be addressed: Ensuring FOH (Front of House) Connectivity: Each location had to be properly connected to its local APPIX server to ensure real-time content delivery. Cue Synchronization with Ableton Live: Each Highlands Church location triggered its respective cues using Ableton Live, making it crucial for APPIX to integrate seamlessly with this setup. Audience Content Pack Distribution: To ensure each attendee had access to the right content, a robust strategy was required to facilitate downloads of location-specific content packs. The success of this step was made possible through strong collaboration between APPIX and local production teams. Preventing WebSocket Cue Conflicts: With multiple locations operating simultaneously, it was critical to ensure that web socket cues from one venue did not interfere with another. This challenge was overcome, ensuring that each audience received the correct localized content at the right time. Through technical expertise and strategic collaboration, APPIX effectively synchronized the second-screen experience across all 18 locations without disruption.
