Ultimate Fighting Championship

2018 - 2019

Project Scope

The UFC (Ultimate Fighting Championship) is a professional mixed martial arts (MMA) organization that promotes and hosts some of the world's top MMA fighters.

During 2018 and 2019, Appix joined the UFC on their tour of the USA, providing their audience with an enhanced second-screen experience as the event unfolded live in front of them. APPIX was present with the UFC from Fight 227 to Fight 245.

Our Approach

Working closely with the event production team, APPIX was instrumental in bringing live statistics to the audienceâ€™s phones via the UFC proprietary Fightmetric API. Stats were integrated into the customized creative content for each event, and the show producer determined which creative/stats experience would be relevant for each moment during a fight.

Additionally APPIX deployed an end-to-end production solution that was timed to various songs between matches. Brilliantly emotive colours from phone displays, including time-synced phone flashes , brought an extra level of spectacle to the event.

APPIX deployed an antenna array suitable to cover each arena. All cues and communications were controlled from FOH (Front Of House).
