Disney - Encanto Broadcast

2022

Project Scope

The enchanting world of Disney's "Encanto" came to life in a live performance at the iconic Hollywood Bowl. As the show was slated to be featured on Disney+, the stakes were high to ensure a flawless and immersive experience for both the in-person audience and viewers at home.

With unreliable Wi-Fi and cellular connections at the Hollywood Bowl, traditional methods of crowd interaction and engagement would fall short. A unique solution was required to captivate the audience and enhance the production value for the Disney+ broadcast.

Our Approach

Capitalizing on its robust platform, APPIX was brought on board to orchestrate innovative crowd effects for the concert. Bypassing the need for Wi-Fi or cellular connections, APPIX's technology engaged the audience in real-time, making each individual a part of the show's magic and ensuring a cohesive, enhanced visual experience for the broadcast.

The result was a mesmerizing integration of the audience into the storytelling of "Encanto," seamlessly complementing the on-stage performance. The APPIX-driven crowd effects not only intensified the live experience for attendees but also amplified the spectacle for Disney+ viewers.

This successful collaboration reinforced APPIX's capability to elevate production standards, even in challenging environments, and now value proven for entertainment broadcasts by the global leader in entertainment and engaging audience experiences, Disney and DIsney+.
