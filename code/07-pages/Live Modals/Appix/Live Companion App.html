Appix Live Companion App

2016

Project Scope

In 2016, APPIX Technologies Inc. introduced a groundbreaking mobile app designed to enhance live production experiences by allowing operators to control phone functions in real time—without requiring cellular data or Wi-Fi. This innovation created a new paradigm for audience engagement, unlocking immersive possibilities at concerts, sporting events, and public gatherings. Building on this success, in 2021, APPIX expanded its capabilities by introducing a web socket version of its control system, enabling live producers to manage second-screen interactions seamlessly. This advancement not only broadened accessibility but also solidified APPIX as a leader in live event technology. With its unique transmission technology and niche market positioning, APPIX required a tailored approach to design, implementation, and integration to ensure seamless functionality across diverse live event environments.

Our Approach

Rather than developing a one-size-fits-all platform, APPIX adopted a bespoke approach, crafting both the core app experience and customized solutions for each client. This led to a new, distinct department in live production focused exclusively on audience interactivity through mobile technology. Key Innovations: Custom Integration for Each Event: APPIX worked closely with producers, lighting designers, and event organizers to synchronize audience participation with live performances. Seamless Offline Connectivity: Through proprietary beacon transmission technology, APPIX ensured that large-scale audiences could receive time-sensitive content without dependence on traditional networks. Enhanced Engagement Strategies: Beyond technical capabilities, APPIX provided marketing and content strategy support to maximize adoption and participation rates.

Real-World Applications

APPIX has been successfully deployed in diverse environments, including: Live Concerts: Events such as Disney/Pixar’s Coco Live in Concert at the Hollywood Bowl leveraged APPIX for synchronized crowd lighting and interactive moments. Sports Arenas: The Cleveland Cavaliers integrated APPIX into their in-game experience, aligning crowd participation with stadium lighting and entertainment sequences. Safety & Public Events: At Tallmadge, Ohio’s massive community event, APPIX enhanced emergency communication protocols while engaging attendees. Theatrical & Broadcast Productions: Encanto Live at the Hollywood Bowl and the Tokyo 2020 Olympics utilized APPIX to deliver seamless second-screen experiences to live and at-home audiences alike.

Results & Impact

APPIX’s ability to operate independently of Wi-Fi or cellular networks has positioned it as an industry disruptor, providing unparalleled engagement opportunities in environments where traditional connectivity is unreliable or inaccessible. Key Achievements: Expanded Adoption: Used in major live events across entertainment, sports, and safety sectors, with audience participation rates exceeding 50% in some cases. Technical Validation: Successfully deployed across diverse environments, from outdoor festivals to stadium arenas and high-profile corporate events. Revenue-Generating Capabilities: Clients have leveraged APPIX’s interactive platform to create new revenue streams through sponsorships and premium content integrations.

Conclusion

The APPIX Live Production Second Screen Companion is more than just an app—it represents a transformative shift in live event engagement. By seamlessly merging cutting-edge technology with creative storytelling, APPIX has redefined how audiences connect with live experiences. As live production continues to evolve, APPIX remains at the forefront, pioneering innovative solutions that enhance interaction, communication, and spectacle across global events. With its proven track record and continued advancements, APPIX is setting a new standard for second-screen audience engagement.
