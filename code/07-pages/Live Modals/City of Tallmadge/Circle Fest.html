City of Tallmadge - Circle Fest

2023

Project Scope

In Tallmadge, Ohio, an annual community event has grown significantly, now outnumbering the city's population. The organizing committee, made up of public and private representatives, focuses on ensuring safety due to weather, lost children, and security concerns, including the possibility of an active shooter.

To address these challenges, APPIX was brought on board early, going beyond APSA (Area Public Safety Alerts) to provide various additional features for public information, awareness and security. The APPIX platform operate without relying on cellular data or Wi-Fi, which is essential due to congestion issues at large gatherings such as this.

Our Approach

Already endorsed by the Event Safety Alliance, APPIX had established a strong foundation of trust with City Officials and Emergency Responders. Tallmadge also had a well-structured Public Health and Safety plan, including a full matrix, manual, and clear chain of command.

With APSA, APPIX seamlessly integrated into this Emergency Operations Plan.

Utilizing APPIX patented and patents pending ground-breaking transmission beacons along with the APPIX Anywhere solution, the deployment succeeded despite unusual site layout and challenging topography. APPIX's Bluetooth transmission covered over 1.25 kilometers from each unit, allowing unlimited users to receive crucial notifications instantly.
