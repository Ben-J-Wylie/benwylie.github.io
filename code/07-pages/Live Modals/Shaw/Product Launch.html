Shaw - Product Launch

2021

Project Scope

In 2021, APPIX was chosen to enhance a virtual product release event for Shaw Cable, a leading cable and internet provider. As attendees logged in remotely, the app delivered real-time supporting content to their smartphones, creating an interactive second-screen experience.

This seamless integration kept viewers engaged throughout the demonstration, despite the event being entirely virtual. The project highlighted APPIX’s ability to drive immersive digital experiences beyond live in-person events.

Our Approach

This event marked the first time our system was deployed without the use of physical antenna hardware, leveraging APPIX’s virtual capabilities to engage audiences remotely.

Real-time event cues were triggered simultaneously to all at-home audience members, controlled directly from the remote operator’s console.

By adapting our technology for a fully virtual setting, APPIX extended its interactive platform beyond the physical venue, creating an innovative and immersive digital engagement experience for Shaw’s audience.
