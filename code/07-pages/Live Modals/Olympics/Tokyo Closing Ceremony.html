Project Scope

For the 2020 Olympics in Tokyo (held in 2021 due to COVID-19), APPIX was hired to create a dynamic lighting experience at the Olympic Stadium. With no live audience in attendance, the multi-language white-labeled app synchronized athletes' phones to illuminate the stadium floor, transforming their devices into a coordinated light show.

This unique activation allowed APPIX to showcase its cutting-edge technology in a high-profile global event, despite the challenges of the pandemic. The project demonstrated the platform’s versatility in creating immersive experiences, even in unconventional settings.

Our Approach

APPIX phone content was triggered directly from the venue’s media servers, synchronizing with pre-mapped CGI effects designed to composite onto the broadcast feed. This real-time integration created an immersive and engaging experience for the remote television audience.

The client managed the APPIX platform in Japan without requiring on-site APPIX staff.

By leveraging our technology, the television broadcast composite delivered a spectacular effect—bridging the gap between physical and virtual engagement. It redefined the way audiences at home experience live sports in an unprecedented time.
