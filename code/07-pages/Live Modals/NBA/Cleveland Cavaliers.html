Project Scope

APPIX was hired by the Cleveland Cavaliers NBA team during the 2023–2024 season to enhance the audience experience with an interactive second-screen platform.

The app synchronized lighting effects and real-time content on fans' smartphones, creating an immersive atmosphere that ran alongside the game.

The activation took place at Rocket Mortgage FieldHouse, with the venue itself serving as a key sponsor of their bespoke white-labeled app. This collaboration showcased APPIX’s ability to deliver large-scale, real-time audience engagement without the need for Wi-Fi or cellular connectivity.

Our Approach

A central control server was installed to receive MIDI cue signals directly from the Cavaliers' lighting console, ensuring precise synchronization with their preprogrammed lighting cues. Two antenna units, strategically positioned in the catwalks above the basketball court, provided robust signal coverage throughout the arena, delivering real-time interactive content to fans.

Triggered alongside lighting effects, APPIX delivered dynamic second-screen experiences tied to gameplay highlights and entertainment moments, amplifying audience engagement. This integration created a cohesive and immersive in-game atmosphere, extending the energy of live action directly to fans' smartphones.
