Project Scope

MDLBeast Soundstorm, hosted in Riyadh, stands as one of the premier music festivals in the region, celebrating a confluence of global and local music talents. With its vast scale and diverse attendee base, the event demanded an innovative solution for both captivating audience engagement and robust safety messaging.

Given the magnitude of MDLBEAST Soundstorm, there was a pressing need for a system that could enhance the audience's experience while also ensuring timely safety communications. Crucially, this system had to operate independently of cellular or Wi-Fi networks, given the potential for connectivity issues in such large-scale events.

Our Approach

APPIX utilized its advanced engagement platform, making each smartphone an interactive event accessory, enriching the atmosphere and fostering shared experiences.

Prioritizing safety, APPIX introduced a messaging system unaffected by common network issues, ensuring attendees received timely safety alerts without needing cellular or Wi-Fi connections.

Thanks to APPIX's innovations, MDLBEAST Soundstorm elevated its event standards, delivering immersive experiences while ensuring message reliability, thereby reinforcing APPIX's standing in event technology.
