Project Scope

In 2018, the Los Angeles Philharmonic celebrated its 100th anniversary with a grand event at the Hollywood Bowl, one of the most iconic venues in the world. The night featured performances by an extraordinary lineup of artists, including Katy Perry, Herbie Hancock, and John Williams, each sharing the stage with the LA Philharmonic. The Hollywood Bowl's massive proscenium was illuminated with mesmerizing media content, providing a visually stunning backdrop for the event. APPIX was brought in to enhance the audience experience, delivering synchronized second-screen content and immersive flash luminaire effects that would bring the audience into the heart of the celebration.

Our Approach

To create a seamless and engaging experience, APPIX implemented a strategic plan: Broadcast Hardware Installation: APPIX deployed its proprietary broadcast technology at the Hollywood Bowl to ensure consistent and reliable engagement throughout the venue. Real-Time Show Integration: APPIX functioned as a full-fledged production department, with an on-site operator integrated into the production team via ClearCom. This ensured that all phone content cues were executed in perfect sync with the live performances. Audience Participation Strategy: To maximize user adoption, APPIX ambassadors were stationed at venue entrances to inform and educate attendees as they arrived. Additionally, printed materials with instructions were placed on every seat. Interactive Audience Activation: The event’s on-stage MC guided the audience through the APPIX experience, including an audience-wide test activation. The collective gasp of amazement from attendees confirmed the immediate impact of the technology.

Results & Impact

The LA Philharmonic’s 100th Anniversary celebration was a resounding success, with APPIX playing a key role in elevating audience engagement. Notable achievements included: Unprecedented Audience Participation: The integration of APPIX saw record adoption rates, with thousands of attendees actively engaging with the synchronized content. Immersive Visual Effects: The second-screen luminaire effects transformed the Hollywood Bowl into a breathtaking, interactive light show, reinforcing the grandeur of the event. Seamless Production Execution: APPIX operated flawlessly as part of the broader event production team, demonstrating its ability to integrate with world-class live entertainment productions.

Conclusion

The LA Philharmonic’s 100th Anniversary event at the Hollywood Bowl proved to be a milestone in live entertainment, with APPIX at the forefront of audience interactivity. This project showcased the platform’s ability to engage and immerse audiences in large-scale, high-profile events, reinforcing its position as a leader in event technology. The night’s overwhelming success set a new benchmark for future live productions, demonstrating how technology can amplify the shared magic of live music.
