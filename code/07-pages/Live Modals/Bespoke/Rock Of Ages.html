Rock Of Ages

2023

Project Scope

"Rock of Ages", a renowned theater show, graced Toronto with a three-month-long live performance. Given the scale and the audience's expectations, there was a need for an innovative solution to amplify engagement, communicate seamlessly, and enhance the overall theater experience.

The theater's limited Wi-Fi and cellular connectivity posed significant challenges for real-time audience interaction, timely messaging, and distribution of supplementary content. An out-of-the-box solution was needed to circumvent these issues and elevate the audience's experience.

Our Approach

APPIX leveraged its platform to integrate audience smartphones into the show's lighting, creating a unified, immersive experience. Additionally, APPIX offered attendees digital keepsakes from "Rock of Ages" to take home.

Despite connectivity challenges, APPIX ensured attendees received crucial notifications like show timings and intermission alerts. APPIX's specialized communication technology operated flawlessly, even in low-connectivity settings.

APPIX elevated the "Rock of Ages" experience, setting a new standard in Toronto's theater engagements. Attendees enjoyed a deeper connection to the show, while organizers received glowing reviews, bolstering APPIX's standing in event tech innovation.
