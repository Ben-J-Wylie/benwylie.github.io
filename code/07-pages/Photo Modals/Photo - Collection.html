Photo Collection

2006

Synopsis

This evocative photo collection draws inspiration from the powerful social documentary style of Phil Borges, highlighting the humanity and resilience of individuals across diverse global locations—Guatemala, Cambodia, Thailand, and Vancouver.

The striking visuals focus on the contrast between the subject’s natural skin tones, which remain intact, and the often chaotic or desolate environments around them.

Through this juxtaposition, the photographs invite a deeper reflection on the fragility of human existence in the face of societal and environmental challenges.
