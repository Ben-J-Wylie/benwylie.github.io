Subject: Natural Growth / Interference / Letting Things Be As They Are

Synopsis

A man watches excitedly as the seed he planted in the ground sprouts and begins to form a beautiful little tree.

With enthusiasm, the man does everything he can to help the little sapling grow tall and strong, but he finds his hands to be too big and his manner too clumsy for the delicate nature of the little sprout and its tiny buds.

After repeat attempts to make things right for his little tree, the man finds that he has in fact only made things worse. The tree is left broken and on the verge of death.

Sitting back, the man refrains from any further intervention and he let a natural course restore the tree to back to health.
