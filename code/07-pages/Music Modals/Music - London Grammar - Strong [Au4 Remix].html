London Grammar - Strong [Au4 Remix]

2014

Feature

"Strong" is an original track by the British indie pop group London Grammar, released in 2013. The song received positive attention, reaching number sixteen on the UK Singles Chart.

Au4's remix offers a unique interpretation of the original track, blending electronic elements with the song's ambient and trip-hop influences.

Contributors

Dot Major / Hannah Reid / Daniel Rotham: Original composition and performance

Ben Wylie: Remix / Production / Vocals

Aaron Wylie: Remix / Production
