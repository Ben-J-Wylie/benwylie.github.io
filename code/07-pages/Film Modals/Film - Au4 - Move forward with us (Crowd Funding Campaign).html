Au4 - Move forward with us (Crowd Funding Campaign)

2012

Synopsis

This documentary-style crowdfunding campaign video follows brothers Aaron and Ben Wylie on their creative journey, showcasing their vision of moving forward as a collective.

The narrative centers on not just a collective of artists, but a unified community of artists and fans coming together to shape the future of music. The video emphasizes the importance of connection and collaboration, both artistically and technologically.
