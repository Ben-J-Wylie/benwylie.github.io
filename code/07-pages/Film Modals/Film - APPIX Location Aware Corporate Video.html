APPIX Location Aware Corporate Video

2017

Synopsis

This documentary-style video takes viewers on an emotional journey, illustrating the profound sense of connection that audience members experience when attending a live performance.

Through compelling storytelling and intimate footage, the video captures the energy and unity shared by people gathered together in a space, highlighting the magic of live events.

As the narrative unfolds, the focus shifts to how Appix elevates this communal experience, transforming it into something even more powerful. The video builds to a crescendo, showcasing how Appix enhances the emotional and collective experience of live performances.
