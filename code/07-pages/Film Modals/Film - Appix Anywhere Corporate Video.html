Appix Anywhere Corporate Video

2021

Synopsis

In this visually captivating corporate video, Appix showcases its innovative approach to engaging virtual audiences during the COVID-19 lockdown.

The technique uused in this video involves creating the illusion of three-dimensional depth and movement using multiple 2D layers arranged in a virtual 3D space. By positioning flat 2D cards or images at varying depths within this space, a virtual camera is then animated to fly through the layers.

The camera's movement, along with a shifting depth of field, creates a dynamic 3D effect, where each card appears to shift and move relative to the others, mimicking a real 3D environment.
