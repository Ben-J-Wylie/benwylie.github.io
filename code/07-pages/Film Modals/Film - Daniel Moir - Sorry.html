Daniel Moir - Sorry

2014

Synopsis

In this captivating music video, we follow a man as he sits down with his guitar, ready to write a song. However, he’s faced with the frustrating challenge of creative writer's block.

As he reflects in a pensive moment, he abandons the paper in front of him and begins to draw on his hand, an unexpected outlet for his creativity.

The video takes us on a journey as he continues drawing on his arm, his guitar, and even a snare drum, transforming them into vibrant canvases. As the colors intensify, so does his creative flow—he brings out paints and covers his instruments in bold, expressive designs.

By the end of the video, both he and his instruments are completely drenched in paint, symbolizing the release of his creativity and the abundance of inspiration that follows.
