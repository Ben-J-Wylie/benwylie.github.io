APPIX at LA Phil 100 at the Hollywood Bowl

2019

Synopsis

This high-energy sizzle reel highlights Appix's stunning activation at the Hollywood Bowl during the LA Philharmonic's 100th anniversary celebration.

The reel captures the breathtaking "audience-wide phone luminaire" effect, which created a dazzling visual backdrop during performances by music legends Katy Perry, Herbie Hancock, and John Williams.

Through quick cuts and flashy composite effects, the edit mirrors the vibrant, immersive impact Appix brings to live productions, turning every audience member's phone into an integral part of the performance experience.
